This has the potential to be Epic.

Hi! Thanks for taking the time to reivew this project. After spending a little time talking with Alex Paschall at ECGC and hearing about the frustrations you currently have with your existing Tricaster setup, I think we might make a great team.

First, I should introduce myself. My name is Cody Wilson, and I'm a gamer, a video hacker, an engineer, and music producer. I talk a bit about myself more on my story page if you're interested. I've been looking for a way to give back to the gaming industry in a way that allows me to make the biggest impact. Gaming has brought me camaraderie, support, and joy, and I would like to be able to pay that forward.

To that end, I believe a broadcasting technology stack I am actively developing could offer Epic a repreive from the limitations of a studio-in-a-box solution while staying well within the budget constraints you have set in front of you. The system I'm building enables a studio to separate the various tasks into individual systems that are purpose-built while retaining the flexibility of a full software solution. I have a living whitepaper that explains the inspiration, background, and goals for this soultion in further detail. I am currently in the middle of a short funding round to purchase a lab environment to test various functions and capabilities of this design, and if my funding goals are met I'm on track to complete initial validation testing in Q2 2017.

I would love to sit down with you to learn more details about your existing studio broadcasting technology stack, your pain points, and if/how this solution could work for you. Please feel free to reach out to me by email or phone - cody@codywilson.co, or 563-676-0694.

Thanks so much for your time! I look forward to hearing from you.
